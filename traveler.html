Problem Research

I started with exploring opportunity spaces that a new service could meet backpacker’s needs. Through a few user interviews,I decided to focus on transportation, to help backpackers have a relax traver in a city.

Experience journey map

I created a Experience Journey Map to illustrate the relevant "touchpoints" that users may encounter about transportation. It foregrounds how the customer feels and what they might be thinking and doing at each stage. The questions users may think/feel guided us to design the experience.

Sketches

Then I did a lot of sketches to explore the possibility of transportation. Finally,I decided to focus on scooter and made some models to test.
