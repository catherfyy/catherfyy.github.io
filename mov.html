MOV Service

MOV is a taxi dispatch application which connect with advanced technologies. I decided to redesign the ride-hailing experience to practice my skills.

My goals were to make it fast and easy to use for everyone, everywhere. Then I test the app with 4 participants to understand the challenged riders faced.


UX Analysis

Then I did some UX analysis.I created scenarios to illustrate the possible interaction scenario.I created user experience map to illustrate the painpoint that users may meet.The questions users may think/feel guided us to design the experience.I made user flows to understand the steps user use the app.After UX analysis,I cleared the problems user faced.
